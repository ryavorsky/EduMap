Abstract. The article is devoted to the effective solution of key problems of improvement of theory and methodology of management of the training process, development of effective means and methods of technical-tactical preparation of sportsmen in judo. Judo is a difficult coordinating type of sports, differing primarily by the speed of simple and complex reactions, high information processing speed and decision-making, concentration and distribution of attention. The studies reveal the important characteristics of the patterns of attack actions execution in judo from the standpoint of biomechanics that need to be considered in the teaching process of training wrestlers – students-judoists of Moscow state pedagogical University and Moscow state forest University. The developed concept of management of long term training of judokas allows to reveal effective means, methods and organizational forms of practice, has given reason to implement the modern management methodology of training and competitive activity of the students engaged in judo.
